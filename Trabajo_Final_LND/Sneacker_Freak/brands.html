Brands

In this section we will show you some of the most famous brands:

NIKE

Nike began in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, founded by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman. It became Nike in 1971 and quickly excelled in the athletic footwear market, with innovations like the Waffle and Air shoes. Over the years, it has been a symbol of athletic excellence and style, partnering with famous athletes and expanding its reach into apparel and sports equipment. Today, Nike is one of the most recognized and successful brands worldwide.

ADIDAS

Adidas, founded by Adi Dassler in 1949, quickly became a powerhouse in athletic footwear. With iconic designs like the Stan Smith and the Superstar, it became a symbol of sports performance and urban style. Over the years, it expanded into apparel and accessories, solidifying its status as a global sports brand.

CONVERSE

Converse, established in 1908, rose to prominence with its iconic Chuck Taylor All Star sneakers. Embraced by basketball players and later adopted by counterculture movements, Converse became a symbol of rebellion and self-expression. Despite ups and downs, it remains a beloved brand worldwide.

NEW BALANCE

New Balance, founded in 1906, gained recognition for its focus on arch support and orthopedic footwear. It later expanded into athletic shoes, becoming known for its quality craftsmanship and comfort. Despite fierce competition, New Balance has maintained its commitment to performance and innovation, earning a loyal following worldwide.

REEBOK

Reebok, established in 1958, gained prominence with its athletic footwear in the 1980s. It became known for its aerobics shoes and later expanded into various sports markets. Despite ownership changes, Reebok remains a respected brand in the athletic apparel industry.
